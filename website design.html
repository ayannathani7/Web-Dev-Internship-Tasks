Who is Frontend developer

A **frontend developer** is a tech professional who builds the visible, interactive parts of a website or application that users interact with directly. Using languages like **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**, they translate design mockups into functional, responsive web pages. They focus heavily on **user experience (UX)**, ensuring the site looks great and works smoothly across all devices and browsers. Essentially, they bridge the gap between graphic design and backend engineering to bring digital experiences to life. visit W3schools website
